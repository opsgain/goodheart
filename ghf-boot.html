Bridging Differences In Human Living

While some of us are lucky to be born, raised and enjoying a respectable standard of living yet there are millions globally who are fighting to collect basic necessities for their survival. Goodheart Foundation urges the lucky humans like you to help some very unlucky children, women and men on the planet. The world has more than enough for everyone.

"Wealth is for Distribution" - Swami Vivekananda, KarmaYoga

We all like to be wealthy and certainly there can be no objection to work hard, earn lots of money and lead a comfortable life. Yet many fellow humans are left behind.

In China, India, Bangladesh, Indonesia, Sri Lanka poorest 10% have seen their share of income fall by more than 15% over last two decades.

Women's wages in Asia are between 70% and 90% of men's. Many struggle to survive as wages are only 25% of the amount required for decent standard of living.
