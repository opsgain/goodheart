- Our Mission -

To improve standard of living of unprivileged humans

About

GoodHeart Foundation (GHF) is a not-for-profit social enterprise using the influence of data science and digital technology to improve standard of living of fellow children, women and men who have not been lucky enough in this world to enjoy a respectable life.

We at GHF, believe that the world has enough resources for all humans on the planet and if we become conscious of disparity around us, we can help all mankind live a respectable life. While we applaud people working hard at their jobs, creating wealth for their loved ones and leading a comfortable life; we also acknowledge that some of our fellow humans have not been so fortunate and lead a desolate life.

At GHF, we realise the disparity in distribution of life essentials, and tackle the problem head-on. It is not going to be easy for us, some would say it is the job of the government but we are committed to use the power of data and technology to monitor and give a respectable living standard to the poor and the unprivileged.

We look upto to Swami Vivekananda's words on KarmaYoga in our journey as the guiding light, for all life is one life.

"It is a worship for him to acquire and spend wealth nobly, for the householder who struggles to become rich by good means and for good purposes is doing practically the same thing for the attainment of salvation as the anchorite does in his cell when he is praying; for in them we see only the different aspects of the same virtue of self-surrender and self-sacrifice prompted by the feeling of devotion to God and to all that is His"

*GoodHeart Foundation is an independent organisation and NOT affiliated to any trust or organisation.

Our Focus

We focus on HEALTHCARE at present. The challenge to improve living is immense and we agree it covers nutrition, education, personal development and many other aspects, but we take one problem sector at one time, perfect our delivery systems and programs for it and move to the next step. We run like a business, and take great insipiration from capitalism and big enterprises.
